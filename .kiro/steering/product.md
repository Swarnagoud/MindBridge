# MindBridge — Product Overview

MindBridge is an AI-powered mental health support platform. It provides users with:

- **AI Chat**: Conversational support with sentiment analysis (stress, anxiety, etc.)
- **Mood Tracking**: Log daily moods with optional notes and visualize trends over time
- **Recommendations**: Personalized suggestions based on chat history and mood data
- **Resources**: Curated mental health resources
- **Tele-Counseling**: Access to remote counseling services

The platform targets individuals seeking accessible mental health support. Authentication is required for all core features. The app is designed for both desktop and mobile (responsive layout with sidebar on desktop, bottom nav on mobile).
